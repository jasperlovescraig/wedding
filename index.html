Our Fairy Tale

We met on vacation in Costa Rica. We both opted to travel with the meetup group random events on a 8 day/7 night all inclusive trip. What better way to meet the love of your life than on a tropical adventurous vacation?!

How did we actually fall for each other? Well you can ask both of us and most likely you will get two different responses. Jasper claims that Craig did not notice her at all the first day or night and that she was the one who came on to him. Craig completely disagrees as he indeed did notice her. His first reaction to her was what a breathtakingly beautiful woman she is. He claims he did not approach her because he thought all along she was unavailable. When he realized this was not the case, he quickly went after her and now he couldn't be happier. She is the love of his life and we are so excited to start our life together. We are excited to share this important part of our life with you all!

    Our Honeymoon

    Our honeymoon was an amazing experience. If there were one word to describe our experience in French Polynesia, it would be "breathtaking." Bora Bora was the most beautiful island we have ever seen. It was truly a blessing to experience staying in an overwater bungalow. Every morning, we woke up with the lagoon as our backyard. It was blissful.

    Things we loved about Tahiti!

    • The amazing sunset and sunrise!
    • Our overwater bungalows!
    • All the exotic fish we fed everyday. There were so many different species. Our favorite was the needle fish. We even saw sharks!
    • The food was exquisite! Our favorite restaurants were La Villa Mahana and Bloody Mary's.
    • We snorkeled, kayaked, and paddleboarded!
    • The calm soothing sounds of the lagoon
    • Having to have to take and boat and plane to go to the other islands
    • No technology!
    • Magic Mountain! We had such a amazing time touring on our ATV!
    • Happy hour

    Take a look at our pics and see for yourself how beautiful Tahiti is.
